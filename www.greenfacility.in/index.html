Our Story

Green Facility began with a simple idea—make housekeeping for organizations predictable, professional, and hassle-free. Today, we support corporates and institutions with trained manpower, standardized checklists, and measurable outcomes across offices and campuses.

Our Mission

To maintain healthy, high-performing environments for employees and students through SOP-driven cleaning, safety-first practices, and continuous supervision—delivered on time, every day.

What Sets Us Apart

Verified and trained staff, on-site supervisors, audit-ready reporting, and flexible deployment for corporate offices, schools, and colleges. We align with your SLAs and keep quality consistent across every shift.
